Milka's corner

Hello my friends! Welcome to my corner. I hope you'll enjoy my pictures as much as I do :)
There's a short story about me.
I am a house cat born in November 2020, so I should be like...2 years old. Fix me if I'm wrong. I'm a cat so calculating is just not my thing.
I like hunting flies and mosquitos, sleeping and playing with toys. But there is one thing I really love. Its pushing various things off the edges. You would not believe how much fun it is! Try it some time and you'll see.
Just because I'm a house cat doesn't mean I don't enjoy outside world. In fact, I enjoy it even too much, to the point where my humans have to put harness on my neck so I would not escape to the wild. That's annoying but I just can't help myself. As soon as I feel fresh air in my nose I cannot control myself anymore. I climb fences and even roofs. You would not expect a house cat to be that cool :)
My most favourite thing in the world?
Oh.. there's so many I cannot decide. Chasing flies? Or that red dot? sometimes all I need is that bright shadow on the wall (whatever it is). If you have some empty boxes, then we have a deal. It doesn't have to be empty though. Its easily fixable. If you want to see more about boxes... go here!
I think I know finally my favourite thing. And its....being the center of attention! I can't be the only one like this, right?

But there is a little secret about me. One minute I can be the cutest thing in the world. The other I will be chasing you around the house (as if you could escape from me :) and you will notice this evil look on my face. My humans think I have double personality but for me it's just a life.

But sometimes I can't shake a feeling that they might be right.
