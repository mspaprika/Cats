White corner

Hello human friends.
I am called White for the sake of simplicity, but my real name is even worse.
My first owners called me Kat which is right, I mean I am cat. Who would argue with that?
But honestly, humans, I feel you could do better with this.
Why not use a bit of imagination when deciding your pet's names. Just saying..
But that's ok with me. Let me tell you about myself.
I have a calm and friendly personality which is really helpful to make human friends. But it is not that helpful with other more agressive local cats. I get into fights and that's just not my thing...
I could hide inside my house, you would say. But it's not that simple when there's 3 toddlers living in the same house.
If there's a choice between 3 toddlers and being eaten by wolves... I'm choosing wolves.
This is how I became outdoor cat.
It's not always comfy here outside but I never regret this decision.
Being a friendly cat I am, I made sure I never go hungry as there's always a human to feed me.
I am able to enjoy some priviliges of a house cat and at the same time enjoy freedom.

When it comes to Milka, situation is a bit complicated.
She's not my type but that doesn't bother me. If she wasn't so boisterous maybe we could be even friends...
Probably she just doesn't want to share a spotlight with me.
This is so silly.
I am wise enough to stay away from her. I think you should too!
